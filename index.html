How does ClearTerms work?

Pairwise comparison

Rather than asking volunteers to read entire documents and flag all of the important terms, ClearTerms breaks the complex task into small parts; individuals only need to compare two items at a time.

Open definition of "importance"

Existing solutions use pre-defined criteria, which may innaccurately reflect what people really care about.

We do not assume a priori what consumers find important. Instead, we let individuals define “importance” on their own terms - and it turns out that people tend to agree.
